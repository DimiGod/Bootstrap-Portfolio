About Me

I'm a second generation Greek-American, and the first in my family to graduate from college. I achieved a B.B.A. in Finance from the university of Wisconsin- Milwaukee, and am currently pursuing a certificate from the Coding Bootcamp at Harvard Extension School- provided by Trilogy Education Services. My passions are business, entrepreneurship, and innovation. I enjoy reading, exploring new places, and trying new restaurants around town.

Favorite Quote

Innovative Ambitions

This last year I brought an idea to life: I incorporated my own company, attained 6 city licenses and permits (on top of registering with necessary State and Federal organizations). I pitched to over 30 venture capitalists, raised tens of thousands in investments, created a brand and company image, made an operations plan and constantly tweak towards maximum efficiency. I’ve been able to use my company as a catalyst for growth and change in Milwaukee’s

“innovator and startup” community; by partnering with several different organizations (particularly within the University of Wisconsin- Milwaukee). All of that happened while completing my senior year studying undergraduate Finance, and working full time growing and managing the existing company I’ve been working for. A lot can happen in a year, so where will this next year take me?
